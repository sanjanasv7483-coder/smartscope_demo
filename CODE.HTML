Turn your smartphone into a pocket microscope

SmartScope is a demo website UI for an app that helps visualize slides placed under a clip-on microscopic lens attached to a smartphone camera. Use it for learning, microscopy demos and prototyping imaging workflows.

Important: This website and the app demo are for education and research prototyping only. Not a medical device. Do not use for clinical diagnosis.

Quick setup

  1. Fix the clip-on microscopic lens over your phone camera.
  2. Place a prepared microscope slide on a stable, well-lit surface and secure the phone above it.
  3. Enable the phone's flashlight to illuminate the sample for clearer viewing.
  4. Open the Demo > Allow camera permission > Use zoom/brightness controls.

Features

Live imaging

Use your phone camera in real-time to view slides. Zoom and scale controls mimic microscope magnification. The built-in flashlight provides stable illumination.

Capture & annotate

Capture stills for later review and add notes or tags to saved images in the app (demo UI only).

Measurement tools

Measure object size on-screen with a calibrated overlay (requires calibration with a slide scale).
